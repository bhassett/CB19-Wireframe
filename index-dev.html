Stockholm Collection

Scandinavia continued to be a source of inspiration for founder Stefanï Grosse this season, with its understated style and functional beauty. The soft, grey-blue sky offset by vibrant candy-coloured villas on Stockholm’s streets played its role when choosing the collection’s colour palette; including Slate grey, Palma blue, neon Flash Pink, and spirited Berry. To emphasise these contrasting tones, a graphic floral motif was poetically placed on pieces taking inspiration from striking poppy flowers found in bloom during Sweden’s Midsummer.
